Private/Advanced Lessons

These lessons are for surfers that have accomplished beginner lessons and want to progress to more challenging opportunities. Examples of areas to develop include how to surf the green part of the wave, going down the line and learning advanced turns.

Our instructors are all very experienced surfers and have techniques to help you hone your skill. We, at Celtic Surf School, will try and enhance your strengths and help you use them to your advantage to get you catching more waves and help you progress further to becoming a well rounded surfer. Depending on your ability and the conditions, the instructor will pick a board most suited to you. Patience, motivation and analysis are the best and fastest way to progress your wave riding ability. Contact us about the advanced/private lessons.
