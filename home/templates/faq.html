FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got answers. If you have some other questions, call or send us a message!

What to bring to lessons?
We supply everything needed for surfing. But you will need a towel, togs and optional bottle of water. Just keep in mind, glasses cannot be worn and goggles are not advised.
Do I need to be a strong swimmer?
No, lessons are in waist deep water and all our instructors are qualified lifegaurds.
Is there somewhere to change/bathroom?
There is local bathroom, or back of the van (to change only!)
How long are lessons?
2 hours
What happens if I need to cancel?
Please ring in advance of cancellation.
What time are the lessons on?
Lessons are tide dependent everyday so keep an eye on the noticeboard or contact us directly. We cannot run lessons 2 hours before and 2 hours after high tide.
What Equipment is provided?
Surfboards, wetsuits and optionally boots.
